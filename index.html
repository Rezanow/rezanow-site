How to Play

Russian Reserve is a Russian Solitaire variant with free reserve cells and a custom tableau layout.

Objective

Move all cards to the foundations, building each suit in ascending order from Ace to King.

Tableau Rules

The custom tableau columns are your main working area.

  • You may select any face-up card in a column.
  • When you move a card, every card stacked on top of it must move with it.
  • Only the card you selected must be valid for its destination. The cards above it are not checked.

Example

K♠
9♥ ← selected
7♦
3♣
2♣

Selecting 9♥ moves:

9♥
7♦
3♣
2♣

Only 9♥ must legally fit where you place it.

Building on the Tableau

Cards must follow the game’s build rule (e.g., descending by suit).

Empty columns may only be filled with a King.

Foundations

  • Start with an Ace.
  • Build upward by suit: Ace → 2 → 3 → … → King.
  • Cards placed in the foundation cannot return.

Reserve Cells

Reserve cells are free single-card holding spaces that help unblock the tableau.

  • One card per reserve cell.
  • Only the top card of a tableau column may be moved into a reserve cell.
  • Cards in reserve cells may later move to the tableau or foundation if legal.

Use reserve cells to temporarily free blocked cards and create new options in the custom tableau layout.
