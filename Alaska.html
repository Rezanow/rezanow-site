Rules: Alaskan Solitaire

Objective: Build 4 foundation piles up by suit from Ace to King.

Tableau: Build by suit, ascending OR descending. Kings cannot go on Aces, Aces cannot go on Kings.

Moving Groups: Any face-up card can be moved, bringing all cards on top of it. The moving stack does not need to be sequenced; only the bottom card of the move must form a valid sequence with the target.

Empty Columns: Only Kings (or stacks starting with Kings) can be placed in empty tableau columns.

Free Cells: 3 cells hold a single card each. Moves to cells must be single cards.

Auto-move: Tap a card to auto-move it to a valid destination.
